STEPS TO CREATE A GOOGLE CLOUD ACCOUNT 
Step-by-Step Guide to Creating a Free Google Cloud Account
Go to Google Cloud's Free Tier Page:

Open your web browser and go to https://cloud.google.com/free.
Click on the "Get Started for Free" button.
Sign in with a Google Account:

If you already have a Google account, sign in.
If not, create a new Google account.
Accept the Terms and Conditions:

Read and agree to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy to proceed.
Enter Account and Contact Information:

Select your country and account type (choose "Individual" unless you’re signing up for a company).
Fill in your name, address, and phone number.
Verify Your Phone Number:

Enter your mobile number and verify it via an OTP sent by Google.
Enter Payment Information:

Provide a valid credit or debit card for identity verification.
Note: Google Cloud offers a $300 free credit for 90 days, and you won’t be charged unless you manually upgrade your account.
Complete the Setup:

Select No auto-upgrade to stay within the free trial.
Click on "Start my free trial" to finalize the account creation.
Access the Google Cloud Console:

Once your account is set up, go to the Google Cloud Console to start using GCP services.
